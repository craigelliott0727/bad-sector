BAD SECTOR - IMPLEMENTATION MAP
===============================

MAGNET CONCEPT
The magnet is both a recurring environmental hazard and the final antagonist. On magnetic stages,
the player is pulled toward or away from the center as polarity changes. Magnet enemies exert their
own localized pull. The final stage, THE BIG MAGNET, uses several high-health magnets as a boss wave.

WHY THIS FITS THE FLOPPY LIMIT
Nearly everything is represented as short stage tables, simple geometry, compact state machines,
and generated audio. Adding levels, enemy behaviors, text, and music patterns costs bytes rather
than megabytes. There are no bitmap, WAV, font, runtime, or engine files.

REQUIRED BEFORE SUBMISSION
1. Compile with 32-bit MinGW-w64 and fix any compiler-specific warnings/errors.
2. Play all twenty stages and tune spawn rates, timers, utility charges, and boss health.
3. Verify clean launch on both 32-bit and 64-bit Windows where available.
4. Confirm the complete decompressed submission stays below 1,474,560 bytes.
5. Add a tiny in-game credits/help panel and final contest metadata once known.
6. Consider UPX only if the rules allow it; the game should fit comfortably without it.
