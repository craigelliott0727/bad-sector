BAD SECTOR v0.6 DESIGN NOTES

The opening sequence is deliberately procedural. It does not contain a video
or bitmap and therefore adds very little to the final executable.

Tutorial progression:
1. Boot Sector: exactly 12 ordinary damaged sectors, no spreading, one very
   slow delayed virus, persistent partial repairs.
2. Directory Table: more sectors and slow viruses, still no spreading.
3. Hidden Files: introduces additional enemy behavior.
4. Fragment Field: introduces tougher repair types.
5. Magnetic Leak: first magnetic-field lesson.

The first level should now read as a complete restoration: every visible red
sector is part of the goal, the remaining counter reaches zero, and the disk
surface visibly becomes clean.
