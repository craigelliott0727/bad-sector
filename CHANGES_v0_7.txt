BAD SECTOR v0.7

PLAYTEST CHANGES
- Rebuilt the opening animation so the floppy visibly enters a drive, the drive reads it, the camera zooms onto the circular magnetic media, and the sector grid becomes the gameplay surface.
- Added a dedicated TRACK RECOVERED screen between missions with score bonus, story consequence, next objective, and any newly introduced sector type.
- Greatly softened the jump from Track 1 to Track 2.
- Tracks 1-3 do not generate spreading corruption.
- Early enemy speed, spawn delay, spawn frequency, and maximum enemy count now increase gradually.
- Track 2 has at most two slow viruses and 14 sectors.
- A new sector style is introduced with an explanation during each of the first six mission briefings.
- Added on-screen reminders for red, orange, and purple sector behavior during the first moments of their introduction levels.

SECTOR PROGRESSION
1. Red / Corrupt: standard one-second repair.
2. Orange / Fragmented: approximately twice the repair time.
3. Purple / Infected: releases a virus when completed.
4. Yellow / Unstable: may spread damage to a neighbor.
5. Green / Encrypted: can only be repaired during its bright pulse.
6. Blue P / Protected: slow repair, high bonus value.

Partial repair progress remains when the player leaves every sector type.
